BirdCast Alerts provided by [Cornell Lab of Ornithology | BirdCast](https://alert.birdcast.info)

Photometrics AI uses data licensed from [BirdCast](https://birdcast.org), a platform managed by the Cornell Lab of Ornithology to observe and predict bird migration using radar, BirdCast.org. The forecast model was created by B.M. Van Doren (University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign) and K. Horton (Purdue University), as described in Van Doren & Horton (2018). Any opinions, findings, and conclusions or recommendations expressed in this material are those of the author(s) and do not necessarily reflect the views of the Cornell Lab of Ornithology.

**Citation:** Van Doren, B. M., & Horton, K. G. (2018). A continental system for forecasting bird migration. *Science*, 361(6407), 1115-1118.
