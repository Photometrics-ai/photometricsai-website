_Last updated: April 6, 2026_

Photometrics AI ("we," "us") operates photometrics.ai. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it. We keep things minimal on purpose.

## What we collect

- **Analytics.** We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors find and use the site. Google Analytics records standard web traffic data (pages viewed, approximate location, device type, referrer) with IP anonymization enabled. We do not link this data to individual identities.
- **Contact form submissions.** When you fill out a contact or demo request form, we receive the email address and message you provide. That information goes to our inbox so we can respond to you.
- **No other cookies.** We do not set advertising, retargeting, or third-party tracking cookies beyond what Google Analytics uses for its own measurement.

## How we use it

We use the information above to operate the site, improve content, and reply to inquiries. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing.

## Data requests

To request access to, correction of, or deletion of any information you have shared with us, email **ari@photometrics.ai**. We will respond within 30 days.

## Children

The site is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

## Changes

If we update this policy we will revise the "Last updated" date above.
