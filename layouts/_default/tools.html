Nautical Twilight Schedule

Generate a yearly streetlight operating schedule for any location. Returns daily nautical dusk (lights ON) and nautical dawn (lights OFF) times.

The placeholder coordinates (36.7456, −93.4712) are the US Census population-weighted centroid for the United States, near Hartville, MO — useful for understanding average streetlight operating times across the country. For regional analysis (e.g., crashes in Kansas or the Denver metro area), use a state or metropolitan area population-weighted centroid from the same Census reference. Streetlights turn ON at −6° sun elevation (end of civil twilight) and OFF at −6° (start of civil twilight).
